Our machinery

Milling machines

Our milling department is equipped with the latest high-speed machining centres, with 3 and 5-axis numerical control, suitable for various types of processing and materials, from resin to the most resistant metals (cast iron, steel, aluminium). Working area up to 6,000 × 3,000 × 1,500 mm.

Presses

GIGANT 1250-tonne hydraulic press, table size 5000 × 2500 × h 2500 mm, stroke 1500 mm. Müller 400-tonne hydraulic press.

Lathes, grinders and workshop equipment

Lathes, grinders and various workshop equipment for every machining and finishing need.

DEA measuring machine

Measuring dimensions of 3300 × 2000 × 1500 mm, with PC DMIS Hexagon software for precision dimensional control.

Overhead cranes

Various load capacities, up to a maximum of 25 tonnes.
